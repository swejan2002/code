About Us


Our History and Principles

For more than three decades, Property has been dedicated to providing the residents and property owners of Denton County and the surrounding communities with high quality service and an expansive selection of properties. Property’s leadership coupled with the expertise and diligence of our experienced staff has allowed us to grow into the leading residential leasing and property management company in the area.

Property is a wholly independent agency that is driven by the values of quality, professionalism, and integrity, because we know that our clients deserve the best. Our agents provide highly personalized service to each and every client in order to help residents and property owners alike. We are invested in enriching our shared community through client satisfaction, and therefore operate with the highest degree of professionalism. When residents and property owners are happy, our community becomes a more positive and fulfilling place to live, work, and enjoy.

Our selectin of properties is unrivaled by any other real estate company and includes apartments, townhomes, duplexes, houses, and more to ensure residents can find a property that perfectly suits their lifestyle and budget. Because our selection of properties is so expansive, the Property staff is highly knowledgeable about the leasing and management of a variety of property types. Additionally, we have experience in serving many different resident communities, from college students to working professionals.
